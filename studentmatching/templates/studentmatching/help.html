Getting Started:

In order to use HoosThere, you must first provide your email and a password. Navigate through HoosThere by clicking on the different categories at the top of the screen. Your dashboard is the central hub from which you can view and edit all of your profile information, see recent posts, and perform site-wide keyword searches.


Connect with Peers:

To connect with other UVA students, navigate to the User Search page by clicking on the User Search buttton at the top of the screen. From there, you can search the HoosThere community, send others messages, and view your peer's profiles!


View and Create Posts:

Click on the Bulletin tab to view a centralized list of all user posts. Each post contains tags which can be clicked to view more posts of the same category/theme. The bulletin search bar can also be used to explore posts by author or category.

Create a post by simply navigating to the Bulletin page, clicking on the "Create a Post" button, and entering the title and contents of your post. Your post time and category will automatically be displayed.


Send Messages:

Private Messages can be sent and received via the Messaging tab.
